Phenotypic traits, together with speciation and extinction processes are fundamental to our understanding of evolution over long time scales (macroevolution).
I use probabilistic models of trait and diversification dynamics on phylogenies as my main research tool.
I am both interested in testing empirical macroevolutionary hypotheses, and in developing new methods and software that are designed for testing the hypotheses.

I recently completed my doctoral degree at the LMU-München, Germany. Check out the [research](https://kopperud.github.io/research/) tab for an overview of current topics.
